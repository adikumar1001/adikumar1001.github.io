Cleaned errors (age >100), removed near/zero-variance features, dummy-encoded categoricals, split 70/15/15 train/validation/test. Best overall: Random Forest (Accuracy 92.9%, Sensitivity 78.0%, Specificity 97.0%, Precision 89.8%). Best AUC: GBM at 0.9716. Delivered RMarkdown report and ROC visuals to help lenders flag high-risk applicants earlier.
